Product Requirements Document (PRD): CoupleLens 1. Product Vision &
Overview 1.1 The Vision CoupleLens is envisioned as the world's first
Relationship Operating System. Our mission is to provide an end-to-end,
intelligent digital platform that empowers couples to navigate every
stage of their relationship from initial commitment to long-term
stability. 1.2 Platform Overview The platform seamlessly integrates
AI-powered relationship assistance with professional, licensed therapist
services. It combines an AI Relationship Assistant, a robust Therapist
Marketplace, Relationship Analytics, Guided Programs, and Couples
Financial Tools. 2. Core Platform Modules 2.1 AI Relationship Assistant
A continuous, context-aware AI companion analyzing recurring issues and
suggesting communication strategies. Supports multiple agent personas
(e.g., Clinical Psychologist, Empathetic Listener) and maintains
long-term relationship context. 2.2 AI Conflict Mediator Structured
environment for conflict resolution. AI analyzes individual perspectives
without bias, detects emotional tones, and generates neutral summaries
with actionable compromises. 2.3 AI Conversation Rewrite Tool A
proactive tool that rewrites draft messages to ensure they are calmer,
more respectful, and emotionally intelligent while preserving the core
intent. 2.4 Relationship Health Dashboard Analytical center tracking
communication scores, trust levels, and conflict trends via interactive
charts and monthly reports. 2.5 Compatibility Assessment Engine
Deep-dive evaluation of financial mindset, lifestyle choices, and
parenting philosophy. Generates detailed reports on strengths and risk
areas. 2.6 Couple Financial Harmony Tool Shared budgeting, joint expense
tracking, and financial personality testing to eliminate money-related
friction. 2.7 Couple Memory Timeline Secure, private social feed for the
couple with photo/video uploads and automated milestone reminders. 2.8

Member): Primary consumer accessing AI tools, health dashboards, and
therapist bookings. 3.2 Therapist: Professional provider managing
profiles, availability, and telehealth sessions. 3.3 Admin: Platform
operator managing therapist vetting, user support, and business
analytics. 4. Revenue Model & Monetization Strategy 1. Premium
Subscription: Fee for advanced AI features and analytics. 2. Marketplace
Commission: Percentage fee from therapy sessions. 3. One-off Purchases:
Compatibility reports and specialized programs. 4. Event Commission:
Transaction fees from workshop ticket sales. 5. Technology
Architecture - Full-Stack: Ruby on Rails - Frontend: Hotwire (Turbo +
Stimulus) + Bootstrap - Database: PostgreSQL - AI: OpenAI API (GPT-4) -
Payments: Stripe & Stripe Connect - Background Jobs: Sidekiq + Redis -
Storage: Active Storage + AWS S3 6. Security & Compliance Page 2
